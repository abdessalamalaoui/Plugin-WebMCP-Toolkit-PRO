# Abdessalam AI Toolkit for WebMCP Is Here: A Smarter AI Action Layer for WordPress, Bloggers, and Food Creators

We are excited to release **Abdessalam AI Toolkit for WebMCP**, a WordPress plugin built to help AI-enabled browsers and assistants understand your website more clearly.

As more people use AI tools to search, summarize, compare, and navigate content, websites need a better way to communicate with those agents. Abdessalam AI Toolkit for WebMCP adds an AI-friendly action layer to WordPress, giving assistants structured instructions, labeled forms, and clear tools they can use instead of guessing from the visual page alone.

For site owners, this means your website can explain itself better. You can define your AI persona, set the tone of interaction, guide agents toward site search, label forms, and monitor recent AI tool activity from the WordPress dashboard.

For bloggers, Abdessalam AI Toolkit for WebMCP helps AI assistants discover posts, understand your niche, and follow your preferred content workflow. Whether you publish tutorials, reviews, resources, news, or long-form guides, the plugin helps AI readers interact with your content in a more accurate and useful way.

For food bloggers, the included **Abdessalam AI RM Addon** brings structured recipe support for WP Recipe Maker. AI agents can access recipe names, ingredients, instructions, nutrition, and servings through dedicated tools. That makes it easier for visitors to ask for recipe summaries, shopping lists, ingredient details, and serving-size adjustments.

The release includes:

- Abdessalam AI Toolkit for WebMCP for WordPress.
- A built-in AI Persona Wizard.
- Declarative form mapping for search, comments, contact forms, and other forms.
- WebMCP tool registration on the front end.
- AI Monitor for recent action-layer activity.
- Help & Docs inside the WordPress dashboard.
- Abdessalam AI RM Addon for WP Recipe Maker sites.
- Structured recipe tools for ingredients, instructions, nutrition, and serving scaling.

Abdessalam AI Toolkit for WebMCP is designed for the next phase of the web: sites that are not only readable by people, but also understandable by helpful AI agents.
